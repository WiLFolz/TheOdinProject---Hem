Garlic Chicken

Description

Garlic chicken is one of the basic one to cook.

Ingredients

  • 700g (approx. 1.5 lbs) boneless, skinless chicken breasts (filleted into thin cutlets)
  • 1 tsp salt, divided
  • 1/2 tsp freshly cracked black pepper, divided
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter, divided
  • 5-6 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 60ml (1/4 cup) chicken broth (or dry white wine)
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken: Pat the chicken cutlets dry with a paper towel. Season both sides with 1/2 teaspoon of salt and 1/4 teaspoon of pepper. Lightly dust the chicken with flour on both sides, rubbing it in to create a thin, even coat.
  2. Sear the chicken: Heat the olive oil and 1 tablespoon of butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook for 4 to 5 minutes per side, until golden brown and cooked through (internal temperature should reach \(165^{\circ }F\)). Remove the chicken to a clean plate and set aside.
  3. Build the sauce: Turn the heat down to medium-low. Melt the remaining 3 tablespoons of butter in the same skillet. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant—be careful not to let it burn.
  4. Deglaze and finish: Pour in the chicken broth (or wine) to deglaze the pan, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom. Let it simmer and reduce for about 1–2 minutes.
  5. Coat the chicken: Coat the chicken: Return the chicken to the skillet, spooning the garlic butter sauce generously over the top. Let it simmer for 1–2 more minutes to heat the chicken through.
